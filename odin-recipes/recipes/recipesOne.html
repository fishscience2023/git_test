Steamed Pork Ribs with Black Bean Sauce

Description

A classic Cantonese dim sum favorite. Tender, bite-sized pork ribs are meticulously marinated and steamed to perfection, resulting in a savory, juicy, and fall-off-the-bone texture.

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs
  • Fermented black beans
  • Garlic
  • Chili
  • ShaoXing wine
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prep: Cut the pork ribs into small pieces and soak them in cold water to remove impurities, then pat completely dry.
  2. Marinate: Toss the ribs with minced garlic, chopped black beans, soy sauce, ShaoXing wine, cornstarch, and a touch of oil. Marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  3. Steam: Arrange the ribs in a single layer on a plate and steam over high heat for 12–15 minutes until thoroughly cooked and tender.
