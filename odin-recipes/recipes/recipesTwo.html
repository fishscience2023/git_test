Hot and Sour Shredded Potatoes

Description

A classic, comforting Chinese stir-fry. Sliced into paper-thin strips, the potatoes are quickly tossed in a wok to retain their signature crisp texture, perfectly balanced with a sharp vinegary tang and a spicy kick.

Ingredients

Russet potatoes, dried red chilies, Sichuan peppercorns, garlic, white vinegar, light soy sauce, and scallions.

Steps

  1. Prep: Shred the potatoes evenly and rinse them thoroughly in cold water multiple times to remove excess starch (this keeps them crunchy).
  2. Aromatics: Heat oil in a wok, infuse it with Sichuan peppercorns, then remove the peppercorns. Fry the dried chilies and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Stir-Fry: Add the potato shreds, stir-fry on high heat for 1–2 minutes, then swirl in the vinegar and salt. Toss quickly and serve immediately.
